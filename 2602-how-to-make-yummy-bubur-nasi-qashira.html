Ingredients of Bubur Nasi Qashira

  1. You need 240 gm of beras basuh bersih dan toskan.
  2. Prepare 750 ml of air mineral.
  3. You need 2 of sudu besar minyak sapi/butter + 2 sudu besar minyak zaitun.
  4. It's 3 ulas of bawang putih cincang halus.
  5. It's 3 of labu bawang besar potong dadu.
  6. Prepare 1 cm of halia cincang halus.
  7. Prepare 2 keping of dada ayam potong dadu.
  8. Prepare 100 gm of daging kisar.
  9. You need 1 of sudu besar rempah kari daging.
  10. Prepare 100 gm of sayur campur.
  11. You need 1 of sudu besar lada sulah.
  12. It's 2 cm of kulit kayu manis.
  13. You need 1 of kuntum bunga lawang.
  14. It's 3 of kuntum bunga cengkih.
  15. You need 5 of biji buah pelaga.
  16. You need 1 helai of daun pandan.
  17. Prepare 1 of sudu besar garam atau ejas sesuai selera.
  18. It's 1 of sudu besar gula.
  19. You need sedikit of daun sup dan bawang.
  20. You need of telur rebus secukupnya.
