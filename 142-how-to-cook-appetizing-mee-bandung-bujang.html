Ingredients of Mee Bandung Bujang

  1. You need of Mee kuning 1/2 bungkus (mee di celup dengan air panas).
  2. You need of Bahagian A.
  3. Prepare of Bawang merah 5 biji.
  4. It's of tomato 2 biji.
  5. Prepare of Cili padi 6 biji (tak nak pedas boleh kurangkan).
  6. It's of Bahagian B.
  7. Prepare of Sos tomato 4 sudu besar.
  8. You need of Cili boh 2 sudu besar.
  9. It's of Sos tiram 1 sudu besar.
  10. It's of Sayur sawi ikut citarasa (nak banyak ke sikit terpulang).
  11. Prepare of Lobak 1 biji (potong panjang2).
  12. You need of Taugeh ikut citarasa.
  13. Prepare of Bebola ikan 5 biji (potong 1/2).
  14. It's of Telur 2 biji.
  15. You need 1 liter of Air.
  16. Prepare of Udang sederhana 10 ekor (kupas kulit jangan buang).
  17. It's of Garam (secukup rasa).

Mee bandung Muar, or simply Mee bandung, is a traditional Southeast Asian cuisine which originated in Muar, Johor, Malaysia. The word bandung in its name is derived from the literal meaning of the word in the Malay language, which means "mixed" or "pairs".

Mee Bandung Bujang instructions

  1. Rebus kulit udang dengan air 1 liter..
  2. Kisar bahan bahagian A dan tumis hingga garing..
  3. Selepas garing bahan A masukkan cili boh hingga pecah minyak..
  4. Kemudian sos tomato + sos tiram + udang. Akhir sekali air rebusan udang.

  6. Seterusnya telur + taugeh. Garam akhir sekali agak2 ikut rasa masing2..
