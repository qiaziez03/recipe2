Ingredients of Kek Pisang Kluster Kayangan (Eggless)

  1. It's 4 of biji pisang.
  2. You need 120 g (3/4 cawan) of gula perang.
  3. Prepare 115 g of butter dicairkan.
  4. You need 120 g of buttermilk (1/2 cawan susu segar + 1/2 sb cuka, dicampur dan dibiarkan pekat) *Uchu ganti dengan 1/2 cawan plain yogurt 1/4 cwn freshmilk).
  5. Prepare 1 of st Esen vanila.
  6. Prepare of Bahan kering.
  7. It's 160 g (2 cawan) of tepung gandum.
  8. Prepare 25 g of (2 sb) serbuk koko.
  9. Prepare 1 of st baking powder.
  10. You need 1/2 of st soda bikarbonat.
  11. Prepare 1/2 of st garam.
  12. Prepare 80 g of belgium callebout chox (kalau takde guna choc chips sahaja).
  13. You need 30 g of walnut/almond dicincang.

Kek Pisang Kluster Kayangan (Eggless) step by step

  1. Cairkan ✅butter dan ketepikan..
  2. Lenyek ✅pisang kemudian masukkan gula. Gaul sebati..
  3. Masukkan ✅butter cair dan ✅buttermilk. Gaulkan sebati..
  4. Masukkan ✅bahan kering termasuk ✅walnut kisar dan gaul sebati. Boleh juga masukkan chocolate chips bersama..
  5. Tuang adunan ke dalam loyang yang dilapik baking paper dan dioles mentega..
  6. Hancur kasar ✅walnut. Campurkan dgn ✅coklat cip. Salutkan bahan topping dgn sedikit ✅tepung supaya tidak tenggelam ketika membakar. Taburkan atas adunan..
