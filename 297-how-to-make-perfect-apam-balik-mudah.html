'Apam balik' is a Southeast Asian fluffy pancake with cream corn and peanuts. This soft pancake which is usually made to order has a thick surface with thin and crispy side. Ingredients of Apam balik mudah

  1. You need of Bahan inti.
  2. Prepare 200 g of kacang tanah.
  3. It's 100 g of gula.
  4. You need of minyak untuk menggoreng kacang.
  5. Prepare of Bahan Kulit.
  6. It's 2 cawan of tepung gandum.
  7. It's 1/2 cawan of gula.
  8. Prepare 1/2 of sudu kecil baking powder.
  9. It's 1/2 of sudu kecil baking soda.
  10. Prepare 1/2 of sudu kecil garam.
  11. Prepare 1 of biji telur.
  12. You need 1 1/4 cawan of air.
  13. Prepare 2 of senduk minyak masak.
  14. You need sedikit of pewarna kuning.

Apam balik mudah step by step

  1. Buat inti dulu ok. Goreng kacang tanah hingga masak. Tos dan buang minyak guna tisu dapur. Kemudian kisar kacang separa hancur. Ketepikan. Gula jangan buat apa-apa. Guna macam tu je nanti..
  2. Campur semua bahan untuk kulit dia. Blend selama 1-2 minit. Sebelum blend tu gaul dulu sikit-sikit bagi blender korang tak stuck ok hehe..
